## Business Problem

SwiftCart Hub, a fast-growing e-commerce company, lacked a centralized analytics system to monitor sales performance, call efficiency, customer satisfaction, and profitability.

The absence of structured reporting made it difficult to:

- Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Identify regional performance gaps
- Evaluate sales team productivity
- Monitor customer sentiment
- Support data-driven strategic decisions

This project addresses these challenges by designing a comprehensive Excel dashboard to provide actionable insights.

---

## Objectives

- Improve sales performance monitoring
- Analyze call efficiency and productivity
- Provide profitability insights (Revenue per Hour, Sales per Hour)
- Monitor customer satisfaction ratings
- Enable data-driven decision-making
- Improve operational visibility

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional and operational sales data, including:

- Salesman
- Product
- Sale Date
- Region
- Hours Worked
- Sales
- Revenue
- Revenue per Hour
- Sales per Hour
- Calls
- Customer Ratings (Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor)

Time period: August to November, 2023  
Total Records: 120

---

## Methodology

### 1. Data Preparation
- Data import into Excel
- Data cleaning and formatting
- Handling missing or inconsistent values

### 2. Data Analysis
- KPI definition
- Excel formulas (Revenue per Hour, Sales per Hour)
- Pivot Tables for aggregation
- Trend analysis (Revenue over time)

### 3. Dashboard Design
- Interactive layout
- Slicers (Product, Region, Month)
- KPI Cards
- Line charts, bar charts, gauge visualization

---

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes:

- **Total Revenue KPI**
- **Total Sales KPI**
- **Average Call Time**
- **Revenue Trend Over Time**
- **Total Calls vs Sales**
- **Regional Sales Distribution**
- **Revenue by Salesman**
- **Client Satisfaction Score (Gauge Chart)**

The interface allows filtering by:
- Product
- Region
- Month

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue peaked in August driven by increased client engagement.
- Germany outperformed other regions in total revenue.
- Salesperson Joe generated the highest revenue.
- Call efficiency positively correlated with revenue per hour.
- Customer satisfaction score averaged 0.75, indicating high client satisfaction.
